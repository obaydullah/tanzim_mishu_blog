You are amazing, stay in touch

Thank you for visiting the official website of Md Farhad Tanzim also known as Tanzim Mishu in Bangladeshi media industry. Tanzim Mishu is a filmmaker, photographer, traveler and scriptwriter. He is widely recognized for shooting documentary films for Bangladesh Army, Bangladesh Govt. and Bangladeshi Beauty. Besides he makes musical films and tv commercials which are most appreciated on social media platforms. Before starting his own production house, he worked for 5 years in television and film production as an assistant director, editor and scriptwriter. In 2010 he started his own video production house named “out of the box films” and he was nominated for the best director in 2015 on channel-i music award. he believes in long-term goals and working hard.
